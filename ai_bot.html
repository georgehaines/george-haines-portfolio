    MicroStrategy AI Bot at Sainsbury's

    Companies First AI Bot

    I led the development of the company’s first AI‑enabled MicroStrategy Bot, creating a new way for colleagues — including Directors of Stores — to access insights quickly and naturally through both text and speech. Building the solution required overcoming complex challenges around LLM hallucinations, refining model behaviour, and training the bot to understand Sainsbury’s business logic, naming conventions and metric definitions with accuracy and consistency. I designed a conversational interface that allowed a Director to simply ask, “Tell me about this store’s performance,” and receive a clear, reliable summary of key indicators for that specific store based on the latest week’s data. The bot not only accelerated access to insights, but also opened the door to wider automation opportunities, demonstrating the potential of AI to support better decision‑making on the ground. By tailoring the experience to real operational needs, I delivered a practical, user‑centred AI product that improved everyday workflows and strengthened the connection between data and frontline leadership.
